How It Works
1. Client creates escrow with delivery conditions and wallet addresses. OWS policy JSON is generated automatically.

2. Freelancer submits completed work description.

3. Claude analyzes whether the delivery matches contract requirements and assigns a confidence score.

4. If approved, OWS policy engine runs all checks: spending limit, chain allowlist, recipient allowlist.

5. Payment signs and broadcasts. Key is wiped from memory. Audit log updated.
